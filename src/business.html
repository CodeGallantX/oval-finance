Eligibility & Requirements

Simple criteria to qualify for our business financing solutions

Basic Requirements

Required Documentation

  • 6 months business bank statements
  • Valid government-issued ID (National ID, Voter's Card, Driver's License, or International Passport)
  • Proof of business address (Utility bill or bank statement)
  • Bank Verification Number (BVN)

Business Information

  • CAC registration documents (if registered)
  • Business owner passport photograph
  • Minimum 6 months in operation
  • Business owner age between 22 and 65 years

Banking Requirements

  • Active business bank account
  • Regular cash flow activity
  • No history of fraudulent transactions
  • No outstanding non-performing loans with other institutions
